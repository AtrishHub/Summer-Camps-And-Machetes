
 


Purity.

You can choose +2 in any two stats, 0 in two stats and -1 in whats left. 

You have an additional stat called purity which is set at a 3.


When you just had sex or were intimate with another character, roll + purity...
If your partner is an NPC:
On a 10: Choose.
On a 7-9: Your partner is doomed
On a 6: You are doomed. 
If your partner is a PC:
Both roll. The lowest wins.

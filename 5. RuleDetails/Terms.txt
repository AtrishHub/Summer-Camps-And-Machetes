Terms.

-Doomed. The next time this person is isolated, they will die.

- What's Really Going On. This place has its secrets. If you can figure out what is really going on here, you will have an advantage.

- The Target. The killer stalks this person. The next time they are isolated, they will have an encounter with the killer. The world seems to want this to happen. 

-Pull. Your influence over other characters. If you have pull, you can add it to a roll relating to that person.
